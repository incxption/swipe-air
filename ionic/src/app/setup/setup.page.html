Welcome to SwipeAir

This tutorial will walk you through the basic concepts of our app and assist you with taking all required preparation steps.

Let's go

Everything is possible

Just swipe up in one of our supported apps, and the photo, video or other content is transferred to your computer.

Grant permissions

First of all, we need some permissions from you. The access you give to SwipeAir is only used for the app to work correctly. We never collect your personal data.

Overlay other apps We need this permission to create the swipe area on your screen to trigger SwipeAir.
Accessibility service We need this permission to extract data from the app you are sharing, e.g. the video you watch on YouTube. This is only done when SwipeAir is triggered.
